# Healora Score - Comprehensive Health Literacy, Knowledge & Practices Framework

## Score Range (0-100 Scale)

---

## 0-20: Emerging Awareness

### Knowledge: Terminology & Research Literacy
- Does not recognize professional terminology
- Unaware of PubMed, Lancet, or peer-reviewed journals
- Relies exclusively on social media headlines and mass media

### Practices: Diets, Body/Meditation & Content Creation
- No regular nutrition, movement, or meditation routines
- Does not create content, run channels, or organize educational events

### Specialists & Pharmacology
- Avoids specialists; relies on self-treatment or ignores symptoms
- Views medications as quick fixes without understanding mechanisms or risks

### Goal Setting
- Goals are impulsive and trend-driven (e.g., "lose weight by summer")
- Lacks specificity, measurability, or realistic planning

### Characteristic Behavioral Markers
- Seeks celebrity-inspired solutions without medical consultation
- Skips routine check-ups
- Acts reactively; pursues immediate results

---

## 21-40: Foundational Knowledge

### Knowledge: Terminology & Research Literacy
- Recognizes 10-20 basic terms (macronutrients, glycemic index, superfoods, adaptogens)
- Aware of PubMed but lacks effective search or evaluation skills

### Practices: Diets, Body/Meditation & Content Creation
- Experiments episodically with simple routines (12/12 fasting, basic stretching, guided breathing)
- Maintains personal notes without strategic consistency

### Specialists & Pharmacology
- Understands specialist roles; seeks consultation only for acute issues
- Knows Ozempic requires prescription but lacks mechanistic understanding

### Goal Setting
- Vague intentions (e.g., "get healthier," "start a blog")
- No deadlines, success metrics, or actionable steps

### Characteristic Behavioral Markers
- Tries short-term experiments; seeks quick fixes
- Begins basic tracking; copies others' goals without personalization

---

## 41-60: Functional Application

### Knowledge: Terminology & Research Literacy
- Masters 50+ terms (microbiome, bioavailability, circadian rhythms, MTHFR, butyrate)
- Can search PubMed by keywords, read abstracts, and identify basic research structure

### Practices: Diets, Body/Meditation & Content Creation
- Maintains consistent routines (3-5x/week tracking, meditation, stretching, chrono-nutrition)
- Shares experiences in small groups or runs mini-challenges for friends

### Specialists & Pharmacology
- Conducts regular check-ups and basic biomarker testing (Vit D, ferritin, TSH, homocysteine)
- Understands medication mechanisms, side effects, and contraindications; prepares for therapy collaboratively

### Goal Setting
- Uses SMART framework (specific, measurable, time-bound)
- Decomposes goals into steps but has limited risk management

### Characteristic Behavioral Markers
- Maintains structured diaries; adjusts habits based on data
- Asks targeted questions to professionals
- Experiments with basic content creation without monetization strategy

---

## 61-80: Critical Engagement

### Knowledge: Terminology & Research Literacy
- Fluently uses 100+ terms; understands cross-disciplinary connections
- Reads full-text studies, evaluates methodology (RCTs, cohorts, p-values, limitations), and compares sources from top-tier journals

### Practices: Diets, Body/Meditation & Content Creation
- Integrates advanced protocols (keto, cyclic fasting, adaptogens, vagus nerve training)
- Runs expert channels/blogs; conducts paid courses (20-100 participants); drafts technical specifications for health apps

### Specialists & Pharmacology
- Works with multidisciplinary teams; coordinates recommendations to avoid conflicts
- Compares drug classes and aligns therapy strategies with biomarker trends under medical supervision

### Goal Setting
- Sets strategic goals with metrics, hypotheses, and risk assessment
- Uses OKR/KPI frameworks; plans for alternative scenarios

### Characteristic Behavioral Markers
- Synchronizes lifestyle, supplementation, and clinical guidance
- Shares insights in professional networks; makes data-driven decisions
- Tests content/product hypotheses; scales efforts with structured support

---

## 81-100: Transformative Leadership

### Knowledge: Terminology & Research Literacy
- Expert-level mastery; translates complex science for diverse audiences
- Publishes reviews, participates in peer review, conducts systematic reviews/meta-analyses, and interprets high-impact journal data for real-world application

### Practices: Diets, Body/Meditation & Content Creation
- Designs proprietary protocols integrating nutrigenomics, microbiome, and endocrinology
- Launches large-scale educational programs (1000+ participants), accredited courses, and AI-driven apps with wearable integration

### Specialists & Pharmacology
- Partners with clinicians and researchers; participates in clinical observations and pharmacogenetic applications
- Publishes case studies with biomarker tracking; advocates multidisciplinary care models

### Goal Setting
- Pursues systemic, transformative objectives focused on scalability, sustainability, and population health impact
- Uses Theory of Change and Impact Mapping frameworks

### Characteristic Behavioral Markers
- Develops and scales educational technologies
- Leads retreats, consultations, and corporate wellness programs
- Contributes to evidence-based standards; builds partnerships, secures funding, and publishes methodological frameworks
