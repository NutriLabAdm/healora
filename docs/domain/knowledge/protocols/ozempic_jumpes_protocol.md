# Ozempic Jumpers Protocol

## Протокол сопровождения приема GLP-1 агонистов (Оземпик)

### Category: Medical Protocol

---

### 1. Goal

**EN:** Manage GLP-1 agonist therapy (semaglutide/tirzepatide) for weight loss with muscle preservation, side effect mitigation, and rebound prevention.

**RU:** Сопровождение терапии GLP-1 агонистами (семаглутид/тирзепатид) для снижения веса с сохранением мышечной массы, управлением побочными эффектами и предотвращением отката.

---

### 2. Red Flags (Красные флаги)

| Metric (EN) | Metric (RU) | Threshold | Action |
|------------|-------------|-----------|--------|
| Muscle loss rate | Скорость потери мышц | >1.5 kg muscle per month | Increase protein to 2.2g/kg + strength training intensity |
| Severe nausea/vomiting | Тяжелая тошнота/рвота | >3 days unable to keep food down | Consult prescribing physician — dose adjustment |
| Acute abdominal pain | Острая боль в животе | Severe, right upper quadrant | Emergency: rule out pancreatitis or cholecystitis |
| Heart rate | ЧСС | Resting HR >100 bpm on GLP-1 | Consult cardiologist, review medication |
| Dehydration signs | Признаки обезвоживания | Dark urine, dizziness, <1L fluid intake | Immediate hydration protocol, electrolyte supplementation |
| Rapid weight loss | Быстрая потеря веса | >2 kg/week for 2+ weeks | Increase caloric intake, preserve lean mass |
| Gallbladder symptoms | Симптомы желчного пузыря | Pain after fatty meals | Ultrasound, consult gastroenterologist |
| Weight regain after stop | Откат веса после отмены | >50% of lost weight within 3 months | Activate rebound prevention protocol immediately |

### 4. Phases (Фазы)

#### Phase 1: Initiation (Weeks 1-4) / Инициация (1-4 недели)

Dose escalation, side effect adaptation, establish baseline metrics.

- Start protein at 1.6g/kg body weight minimum
- Begin strength training (3x/week, full body)
- Log daily: nausea, appetite, energy, bowel movements
- Weigh daily, body composition weekly
- Set caloric floor (never <1400 kcal/day for women, <1800 for men)
- Hydrate 2.5-3L/day with electrolytes

#### Phase 2: Active Weight Loss (Weeks 5-24) / Активное снижение веса (5-24 недели)

Sustained weight loss with muscle preservation focus.

- Increase protein to 2.0-2.2g/kg lean mass target
- Strength training 4x/week (progressive overload)
- Track fat mass vs lean mass ratio weekly
- If muscle loss >1% per month → increase calories + protein
- Blood work every 3 months: iron, B12, folate, vitamin D, zinc
- GI management: ginger tea, small frequent meals (6-8x/day)

#### Phase 3: Tapering & Habit Integration (Weeks 25-36) / Снижение дозы и интеграция привычек (25-36 недели)

Gradual dose reduction while reinforcing lifestyle habits.

- Gradually reduce dose over 8-12 weeks (never stop abruptly)
- Increase caloric intake to maintenance level (~200-300 kcal/day more)
- Emphasize habit autonomy: the user MUST maintain eating habits without medication
- Continue strength training 3-4x/week
- Weekly check-ins with coach/dietitian
- Monitor for emotional eating triggers returning

#### Phase 4: Maintenance / Rebound Prevention (Weeks 37+) / Поддержание / предотвращение отката (37+ недели)

Long-term weight maintenance after GLP-1 discontinuation.

- Continue tracking: weight (daily), body comp (weekly)
- Maintain protein at 1.6g/kg minimum lifelong
- Strength training 2-3x/week minimum
- If weight regain >2 kg in a month → activate emergency intervention
- Monthly check-ins for 6 months post-medication
- Rebound risk: >80% of users regain weight within 1 year without habit change

---

### 5. General Recommendations

1. NEVER skip strength training — muscle loss is the #1 hidden risk of GLP-1 weight loss
2. Protein target 1.6-2.2g/kg — use shakes if food intake is too low
3. Small frequent meals (6-8x/day) to manage nausea and maintain nutrient intake
4. Hydrate aggressively — GLP-1s reduce thirst sensation
5. Track body composition, not just weight — fat loss ≠ healthy loss
6. Plan for medication tapering from day 1 — habit change is the real treatment
7. Gallstone prevention: avoid very low fat diets, include healthy fats daily
8. Bowel management: fiber 25-30g/day + magnesium + hydration to prevent constipation
9. Do NOT start GLP-1 without concurrent strength training prescription
10. Consider creatine (5g/day) for additional muscle preservation support

---

### 6. Contraindications (Противопоказания)

- Personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma
- History of pancreatitis
- Severe gastroparesis
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding
- BMI <27 without type 2 diabetes (off-label use varies by country)
